    You've Never Seen Anything Like This Before

    Innovation. That’s what the Ford GT is all about. From its aerodynamically optimized shape to its multifunctional buttresses to its extraordinarily powerful 3.5L EcoBoost® V6 engine, everything about the Ford GT is designed to deliver pure performance.

    Configurator

    What does your ideal version look like? The Configurator lets you explore all
    the exciting variations of the Ford GT.

    Performance and Technology

    The teardrop shape of the Ford GT is the result of extensive work in the wind tunnel. Its
    carbon-fiber body and its 647-horsepower 3.5L EcoBoost® V6 engine are the ultimate
    expressions of technological innovation.

    Super Light . Super Strong

    The lightweight body is made of carbon fiber . Aluminium structures minimize the weight of the high-strength subframe

    Suspension

    Long suspension arms and inboard damper packaging deliver track-capable vehicle dynamics
    ,an example of Ford Performance engineers exploring suspension design for future Ford Performance vehicles.

    Carbon-ceramic brakes

    To help overall performance , BremboTM carbon-ceramic brakes have bee engineered into the Ford GT.
    Brembo is a trademark of Brembo S.p.A

    An amazingly powerful engine

    Twin-turbocharged , intercooled EcoBoost® 3.5-liter V6 with 647 horsepower
